Scoring Methodology

Proximity Score

Measures how close a target company's locations are to the current platform (Lampton-Love + acquired companies). Each target location scores points based on its distance to the nearest platform location:

  • 10 pts — within 5 miles
  • 7 pts — within 15 miles
  • 5 pts — within 25 miles
  • 3 pts — within 50 miles
  • 1 pt — within 100 miles

County Score

Evaluates the market quality of each target location by looking up the nearest county's estimated propane consumption (within 25 miles). Each location can earn 0-10 points based on the county's consumption relative to the highest-consuming county in the data set.

Total Score

Sum of Proximity + County scores. Higher scores indicate targets that are both geographically close to the platform and located in attractive propane markets.

Score Color Key

Scores are color-coded to indicate acquisition attractiveness:

  • GREEN (30+) — High priority target. Strong proximity and market overlap.
  • AMBER (10-29) — Medium priority. Moderate strategic fit worth evaluating.
  • GRAY (<10) — Monitor. Limited overlap with current platform.
