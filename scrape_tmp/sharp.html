Locations

Sharp Energy sells and distributes propane gas to residential, commercial, industrial, wholesale and autogas customers. Our integrated propane operations in Delaware, Maryland, Virginia, North Carolina and Pennsylvania (Sharp Energy) and Florida (Flo-Gas Company doing business as Florida Public Utilities Company) allow us to provide the highest quality service to existing customers while growing and extending into new markets and service territories. Our propane gas operations distribute propane gas to approximately 91,000 customers.
